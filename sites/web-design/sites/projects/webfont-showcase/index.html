Creator

Raymond Larabie, a font obsessed geek, started Typodermic fonts in 2001 in Canada & moved to Japan in 2008. His specialty is technical, industrial & ultramodern styles but takes swings at all genres.

About

Outstretched pod forms suggest rapid horizontal movement. Sharp diagonal cuts and anomalistic gaps inject your words with nontraditional, scientific sensibility.
